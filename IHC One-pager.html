Apa itu IHC OneKlinik?

What is OneKlinik IHC?

In-House Clinic adalah klinik permanen yang beroperasi di dalam perusahaan Anda — bukan program musiman. Kami menempatkan tenaga kesehatan terlatih beserta semua perlengkapannya, sehingga karyawan dapat mengakses layanan tanpa keluar dari lingkungan kerja.

Seluruh obat-obatan bersumber dari klinik pratama OneKlinik di Sunter — standar dan ketersediaan terjaga. Anda cukup sediakan ruangan, sisanya kami yang urus.

An In-House Clinic is a permanent clinic operating inside your company premises — not a one-time event. We station trained medical staff with full equipment, giving employees direct access to healthcare without leaving the workplace.

All medications are sourced from OneKlinik's primary clinic in Sunter — consistent quality and availability guaranteed. You provide the room, we handle everything else.
